TORONTO OFFICE HOURS

come join us!! every friday at 6:30 pm!!

what?

Weekly public gathering every Friday at Rooms on Dupont Street starting at 6:30 pm. Easy drop-in if you need to bail early for the Rooms dance party or anything else.

who?

Starts with people I've had a good conversation with in Toronto, then anyone they've had a good conversation with, too. Originally, the informal "office hours" concept consists of sending out an invite to your followers on Twitter to meet you at a specific bar. I would like for this to not only unite people who follow me on Twitter, hence the broader reach!

why?

Because Toronto is full of interesting people who should bump into each other more often. A standing, low-key slot makes it simple to keep showing up and slowly turn strangers into a real community!

current location: Rooms on Dupont Street
